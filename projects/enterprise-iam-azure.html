Executive Identity Scenario

An organization is migrating its identity infrastructure to Microsoft Entra. The identity administrator must onboard staff, enable secure vendor access, govern permissions, investigate authentication activity, and maintain audit visibility — all while enforcing Zero Trust principles. This lab simulates that operational lifecycle.
