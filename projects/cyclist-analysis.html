Cyclistic Rider Behavior Analysis

A year-long analysis of Chicago’s bike-share program comparing casual riders and annual members. Supports the Google Data Analytics Capstone objective: converting high-usage casual riders into long-term members.

Key Insights

  • Members ride heavily during weekday commutes
  • Casual riders take nearly 2× longer rides
  • Electric bikes are more popular among casual users

Summary

This Cyclistic rider analysis provides data-driven insights into how casual and member riders behave across seasons, weekdays, and ride durations. These findings help shape marketing strategies aimed at converting high-usage casual riders into long-term members using real behavioral evidence.
