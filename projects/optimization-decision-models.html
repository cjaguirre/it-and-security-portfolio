Linear Programming (LP)

LP models identified the most cost-effective combination of labor hours and materials while meeting operational requirements. 

Maximize: Profit = 40x + 30y

Subject to:
    2x + y ≤ 100   -- Labor hours
    x + y ≤ 80     -- Machine capacity
    x, y ≥ 0

Solver results produced the optimal production mix under cost and capacity constraints.

Binary Integer Programming (BIP)

BIP models addressed yes/no decisions such as project selection under budget constraints. 

Maximize: 12x1 + 10x2 + 8x3

Subject to:
    5x1 + 4x2 + 3x3 ≤ 10    -- Budget
    xi ∈ {0,1}
